## Important Notes

### ⚠️ ngrok Limitations
- **Free tier URLs change** every time you restart ngrok
- For **stable URL**, consider:
  - ngrok paid plan (reserved domain)
  - Deploy to production server with real domain

### ⚠️ For App Review
- Facebook reviewers need **access to these URLs**
- Make sure **ngrok is running** during review
- URLs must be **publicly accessible**
- Consider using **ngrok paid plan** for review process

### ⚠️ Production Deployment
When you deploy to production, update these URLs to your real domain:
- Privacy Policy: `https://yourdomain.com/privacy-policy`
- Terms of Service: `https://yourdomain.com/terms-of-service`
