# Privacy Policy

**Last Updated:** November 29, 2024

## 1. Introduction

Omni CRM ("we," "our," or "us") is committed to protecting your privacy. This Privacy Policy explains how we collect, use, disclose, and safeguard your information when you use our Facebook Messenger integration service ("Service").

By using our Service, you agree to the collection and use of information in accordance with this policy.

## 2. Information We Collect

### 2.1 Information You Provide
- **Facebook Page Information**: We collect your Facebook Page ID and access tokens to enable messaging functionality
- **Account Information**: Email address, name, and other information you provide when creating an account
- **Messages**: Messages sent through Facebook Messenger to your connected Facebook Page

### 2.2 Information Automatically Collected
- **Usage Data**: Information about how you use our Service
- **Device Information**: Browser type, operating system, and device identifiers
- **Log Data**: IP addresses, access times, and pages viewed

### 2.3 Information from Facebook
- **User Messages**: Messages sent by users to your Facebook Page
- **User IDs**: Facebook Page-Scoped User IDs (PSID) for message routing
- **Message Metadata**: Timestamps, message types, and delivery status

## 3. How We Use Your Information

We use the collected information for the following purposes:

- **Service Provision**: To provide and maintain our messaging and CRM services
- **Message Processing**: To receive, store, and display messages from Facebook Messenger
- **Customer Support**: To respond to your inquiries and provide customer support
- **Service Improvement**: To analyze usage patterns and improve our Service
- **Security**: To detect, prevent, and address technical issues and security threats
- **Communication**: To send you service-related notifications and updates

## 4. Data Storage and Security

### 4.1 Data Storage
- Your data is stored securely on our servers
- Messages and conversations are stored in encrypted databases
- Access tokens are encrypted and stored securely

### 4.2 Security Measures
- We implement industry-standard security measures to protect your data
- All data transmissions are encrypted using SSL/TLS
- Access to your data is restricted to authorized personnel only
- Regular security audits and updates are performed

## 5. Data Sharing and Disclosure

We do not sell, trade, or rent your personal information to third parties. We may share your information only in the following circumstances:

- **Service Providers**: With trusted third-party service providers who assist in operating our Service
- **Legal Requirements**: When required by law or to protect our rights and safety
- **Business Transfers**: In connection with a merger, acquisition, or sale of assets
- **With Your Consent**: When you have given explicit consent for sharing

## 6. Facebook Data Usage

### 6.1 Facebook Integration
- We use Facebook Messenger API to receive and send messages
- We comply with Facebook's Platform Policy and Terms of Service
- We only access data necessary for providing messaging services

### 6.2 User Data from Facebook
- We receive messages and user IDs from Facebook Messenger
- This data is used solely for providing messaging and CRM functionality
- We do not share Facebook user data with third parties without consent
